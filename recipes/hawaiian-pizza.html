Hawaiian Pizza

Description

Hawaiian pizza is an incredibly delicious type of a pizza! It consists of pineapple, ham, and cheese, and it gives an unique flavor that is to die for! In this recipe, however, we also include bacon. I'm sure you can agree that bacon goes very well with practically everything. So, without further ado, let's make one!

Ingredients

  • 1 homemade pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup (127g) pizza sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1 and 1/2 cups (6oz or 168g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup (75g) cooked ham or Canadian bacon, sliced or chopped
  • 1/2 cup (82g) pineapple chunks (canned or fresh)
  • 3 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
